# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, engineering leaders, technical peers, recruiters, and collaborators who want a quick read on Zachary Berardo's experience, judgment, and technical range.

## Product Purpose

This site represents Zachary as a personal brand: a staff software engineer with full-stack range, backend depth, leadership experience, and a practical record of shipping software people actually use.

## Brand Personality

Plainspoken, capable, and warm. The site should feel confident without sounding inflated, technical without becoming jargon-heavy, and personal without becoming casual.

## Anti-references

Avoid template portfolio language, exaggerated metric headlines, resume-dump sections, generic developer slogans, startup buzzwords, and copy that sounds like a brand agency wrote it.

## Design Principles

- Use human language before marketing language.
- Ground claims in real work and specific systems.
- Keep the page easy to scan for busy technical readers.
- Let the headshot and experience make the page personal.
- Prefer LinkedIn for current career details instead of hosting stale resume files.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic HTML, clear link text, visible focus states, sufficient color contrast, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion fallbacks for all animations.
